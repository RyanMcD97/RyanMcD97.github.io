What is the project?

For a recent assignment, I designed and created a food mould using a combination of Fusion 360, 3D printing, and vacuum forming, which would be a valuable project for Year 9-10 students. I started by designing the mould in Fusion 360, utilising CAD to create a precise and functional design. After 3D printing the design, I used a vacuum forming machine to produce the final mould from the printed prototype. This project allowed me to explore the integration of digital design with practical manufacturing techniques, showcasing how modern technology can be applied to food production.
