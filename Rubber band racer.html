What is the project?

For a recent assignment, I created a rubber band-propelled race car, which would be an engaging project for Year 7-8 students. The car design involved laser cutting the main body from materials like wood or acrylic and incorporating a 3D-printed component to add a unique element to the build. This project allowed me to develop and apply skills in Fusion 360 (CAD) for designing the car, 3D printing for creating the customised parts, and basic engineering principles to ensure the car's functionality and speed. It was a hands-on experience that combined creativity, technical skills, and problem-solving, making it ideal for introducing students to design and technology concepts.
