What is the project?

For a recent textiles assignment, I incorporated e-textiles using conductive thread to make a banner with lights that illuminate when turned on. I used techniques such as running stitch and hand sewing to integrate the conductive thread into the fabric, ensuring a functional and aesthetically pleasing result. This project provided an opportunity to explore the intersection of textiles and electronics, showcasing how traditional sewing techniques can be combined with modern technology to create interactive and innovative designs.
